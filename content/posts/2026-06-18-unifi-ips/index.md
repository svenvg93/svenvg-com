Unifi's Intrusion Prevention System (IPS) inspects traffic passing through your gateway and automatically blocks known malicious signatures — port scans, exploit attempts, botnet callbacks, and more — without you having to write a single firewall rule. In this post, we'll walk through enabling IPS, choosing the right sensitivity level for a homelab, understanding the threat categories, and reading the alerts it generates.
