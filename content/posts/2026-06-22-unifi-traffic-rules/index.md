Unifi's traffic management tools give you granular control over how devices on your network connect to the internet and to each other. Traffic rules let you block or allow specific connections, application filtering lets you shape traffic by app category, geo-IP blocking lets you drop traffic from entire countries, and policy-based routing lets you steer specific flows through different WAN uplinks or VPN tunnels. In this post, we'll cover all four and show practical examples for each.
