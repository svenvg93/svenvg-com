The Unifi Travel Router is a compact device that connects to any available internet source and tunnels all your traffic back to your home network — so your laptop behaves as if it's sitting on your homelab, no matter where you are. In this post, we'll cover the initial setup, configuring the VPN tunnel back home, and what to expect from the connection on the road.
