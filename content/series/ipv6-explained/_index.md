A practical series that works through IPv6 from the ground up — no assumed prior knowledge beyond basic networking. Covers addressing schemes, how SLAAC and DHCPv6 hand out addresses, how prefix delegation works with ISPs, and how routing and firewalling differ from IPv4. Aimed at homelab users and network engineers making the jump to dual-stack.
